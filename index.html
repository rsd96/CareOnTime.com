Care On Time

An app for your loved ones

Lending a helping hand to make lives easier.

Care On Time is an application that has been designed for IRT, as a service for their elderly at-home care clients. Our aim is to help provide useful information and services at the tap of a screen, to help give them more time to focus on the things they enjoy. The application provides information about future tasks to be carried out, giving the clients a sense of comfort and security. The app will provide notification of any changes to scheduling, reducing stress and anxiety levels for the clients. This application will also allow families to watch over their loved ones, and ensure that every task is being carried out with love and care.

DEVICES

The app will be available on both iOS & Android devices.

CALL

Users can make calls from the app to the nearest IRT office. This saves time redirecting and finding the right person to answer any questions the client may have.

NOTIFICATIONS

The app alerts the user when a caretaker is running late, giving the client peace of mind.

ESTIMATED ARRIVAL

An estimated arrival time of the carer is displayed for the client, creating a sense of comfort and assuredness.

CALENDAR

The application displays two weeks of a client's scheduling information . Each task carried out has it's own detailed information, and the clients can easily request for a change at the press of a button.

CARETAKER

The caretaker's information for each appointment is also available, so the client can know who will be coming before each visit.

Our Demographic

Our app will be available to the thousands of at-home care clients that are serviced by the Illawarra Retirement Trust. These customers are in the later stages of life and generally have very minimal interaction with technology in their day-to-day lives.

iOS / Android

       

Spectra aims to release this application on both Android and iOS platforms, as these are two of the main platforms in wide use in Australia.

IRT facilitates caring for the elderly, and the easiest way to automate many of these services can now be done through the Care On Time app.

This is a great opportunity for Spectra to help so many people manage their day to day lives.
